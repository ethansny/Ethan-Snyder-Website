reach is a theoretical social media/news application designed to connect video game developers with players

In the modern era, players expect communication from developers of games they love. Current methods use existing social media, but this has led to developers being harrassed

This platorm would consolidate social media communication, game functionality, and news into one loaction. Posting and commenting requires linking your account to your game profile to give methods to punish bad actors
